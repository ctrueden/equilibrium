Officially a mining district, the island of Malbolge is the seat of Trell's criminal underworldâ€”a parallel economy running beneath and intertwined with every legitimate business on the other islands. The black markets here deal in everything: illegal goods, stolen artifacts, enslaved labor, and darker commodities that dare not be named in polite company.

[Glasya](../dossiers/glasya) recently ousted the Hag Countess Malagard and consolidated her power. Hags remain deeply embedded in Malbolge's power structure, conducting their own commerce with [Grohnea](../dossiers/grohnea), a witch who harvests and sells soul larvae: twisted beings that are sometimes resold to [Stygia](../locales/stygia) to add to [Levistus](../dossiers/levistus)'s centuries-old stockpile. The official mining operations are merely a thin veil over the true business: slavery, extortion, and the trafficking of souls themselves.
