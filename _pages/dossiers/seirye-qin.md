Seirye Qin is an [SPI](../orgs/spi) agent who disappeared during the
[SPI HQ explosion](../events/spi-fire). She is the sister of
[Bonny Qin](bonny-qin), who impersonated her in a ruse to gain the
trust of the SPI.
