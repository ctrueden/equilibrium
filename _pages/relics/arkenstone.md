A stone sacred to the [dwarven people](../creatures/dwarves), believed to be one of eight divine [relics](.) somehow central to the integrity of reality itself.

Legend says it was forged by [Moradin](../dossiers/moradin), god of dwarves, and used to fuel the fire at the heart of the Great Mountain.

The SPI agents have since realized that the stone was reclaimed by Moradin; given to [Hyperion](../dossiers/hyperion), the Titan of Ice; embedded into the pommel of a sword; and gifted to Hyperion's adopted son [Callisto](../dossiers/cal) prior to sending him out into the world to purge the world of the [undead](../creatures/undead) scourge.
