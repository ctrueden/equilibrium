The SPI was contracted by Arallu's undead factions—represented by the Toreador vampire [Lestache de Lyarcourt](../dossiers/lestache-de-lyarcourt) and the undead lieutenant [Malachai Hawthorne](../dossiers/malachai-hawthorne)—to investigate a catastrophic explosion at [Evershroud Manor](../locales/evershroud-manor). The explosion, caused when the elves cleansed the [Necronomicon](../relics/necronomicon) (the phylactery of the archlich [Timor Invictus](../dossiers/timor-invictus)), had flooded the manor with radiant divine energy that made it inaccessible to the undead and transformed Lady [Ilyana Nightshade](../dossiers/ilyana-nightshade)—who had consumed a Philosopher's Stone centuries ago—into a monstrous creature of chaos. The party fought through the manor's corrupted floors, discovered the [Celestial Hand](../relics/celestial-hand) relic, and descended to the basement where Invictus himself was found in agonized radiant bondage, unable to be destroyed and begging to be killed; he revealed crucial intelligence about the Brucolac's origins and the Flux's nature as unraveling reality. The agents escaped the manor only to be immediately ambushed by the vampire [Malak the Mad](../dossiers/malak-the-mad) and their spawn.
