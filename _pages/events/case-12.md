The SPI was hired by the Holy Sorcerer [Sartus Morningdew](../dossiers/sartus-morningdew) to retrieve the Fruit of Life from the [World Tree](../relics/yggdrasil) and save both the dying tree and [Queen Ambriel Estanesse](../dossiers/ambriel-estanesse), who had been possessed by [Zuggtmoy](../dossiers/zuggtmoy), a parasitic fungal entity that had infiltrated through underground mycelial passages while the court slept. The agents climbed the World Tree, navigated illusions and guardians, and discovered that the druid [Isis Ra'ksh](../dossiers/isis-raksh)—revealed to be a doppelganger—and her gnome accomplice [Peregrine Shackleton](../dossiers/peregrine-shackleton) were blocking access to the Fruit under the influence of the corrupted [Necronomicon](../relics/necronomicon). Rather than steal the Acorn, the party chose to cleanse the source of corruption: they infiltrated the palace treasury, obtained the crystal-sealed Book of the Dead, and performed a ritual with Sartus to erase its evil writings—at the cost of Sartus's life. The agents then confronted the Queen in her throne room, where [Lorissa Stormseeker](../dossiers/lorissa-stormseeker) devised a banishment strategy and an arrow of slaying—fashioned from a page of the cleansed Book—destroyed Zuggtmoy's ooze form, freeing the Queen and saving the World Tree from total corruption.
