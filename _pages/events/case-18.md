Following the grueling Storm Stables mission, the agents spent downtime in [Alye'adu](../locales/alyeadu) and [SPI HQ](../locales/spi-hq). [Mallory](../dossiers/mallory) praised Cal's leadership but rebuked [Freki](../dossiers/freki), [Oz](../dossiers/oz), and [Bec](../dossiers/bec) for conduct during Case 17, and promoted [Cal](../dossiers/cal) to team captain. [Vondal](../dossiers/vondal) received healing from the cleric [Ayina](../dossiers/ayinalef) in Alye'adu and underwent an anchor-bond ritual with the dying changeling Sima, emerging as a new bonded entity. Meanwhile, [Cassandra](../dossiers/cassandra)'s soul extension experiments succeeded, allowing shared spellcasting and contingency magic among linked agents. During downtime the party crafted deadly poisons and rare magical items, and from among the controversial nominees to the Aecus Concord they selected the Toreador harpist [Etria Leduc](../dossiers/etria-leduc) to staff the SPI's Trell branch. The arc began in earnest when [Mallory](../dossiers/mallory) summoned the agents to [Trell](../locales/trell) to meet branch administrator Vex Morradine—with an unnamed client specifically requesting [Callie](../dossiers/callie) by name.
