While attending [Lestache de Lyarcourt](../dossiers/lestache-de-lyarcourt)'s grand masquerade ball in [Sepulchralis](../locales/sepulchralis), the SPI discovered a vampire-orchestrated Hunt—a grotesque entertainment in which humanoids were pursued as prey—and that [Sophie Steelboots](../dossiers/sophie-steelboots), an exiled halfling captain under Tiamat's protection, had been selected as the quarry. To locate and destroy the catastrophic threat underlying the halflings' plight, the agents allied with the Crispic vampire [Goratrix Ceoris](../dossiers/goratrix-ceoris) and ventured into the Soulbound Rest—a huge graveyard anathema to vampires—where they found [Xaryu the Liberator](../dossiers/xaryu-undead), the missing [Xaryu hive component](../dossiers/xaryu-hive), now evolved into a Death Tyrant beholder, growing ever more powerful by consuming the essence of the restless dead. After battling their way through multiple vampire clans (including the near-unstoppable [Enoch the Wise](../dossiers/enoch-the-wise)) and fighting through the defiled [Elysium Sanctum](../locales/elysium-sanctum), the party destroyed the Death Tyrant and narrowly escaped after a clash with the [Brocolac](../dossiers/cronus) himself.
